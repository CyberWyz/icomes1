Frequently Asked Questions

Home / FAQ

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery to university campuses takes 1-3 business days. For off-campus addresses, delivery typically takes 3-5 business days. We also offer express delivery options at checkout for an additional fee.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order is dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and link. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within the UK. We are working on expanding our shipping options to include international destinations in the near future. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when international shipping becomes available.

How do I customize my care package?

You can customize your care package by selecting the "Customize" option on any of our standard packages. From there, you can add or remove items based on your preferences. If you need a completely custom package, please contact our customer service team.

Are your products suitable for people with allergies?

Yes, we offer allergen-free options for many of our packages. When customizing your care package, you can specify any allergies or dietary restrictions, and we'll ensure your package is safe for you. All our products come with detailed ingredient information.

Where do you source your products?

We source our products from local vendors and small businesses across the UK. We prioritize high-quality, sustainable, and ethically produced items. Each product is carefully selected to ensure it meets our quality standards and supports local communities.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely through our payment gateway.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we take security very seriously. Our website uses SSL encryption to protect your personal and payment information. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers. All payment processing is handled by trusted third-party payment processors that comply with PCI DSS standards.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for unopened and unused items. Due to the nature of our products, we cannot accept returns for food items or personal care products that have been opened. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

How do I request a refund?

To request a refund, please contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for the refund. Once your return is received and inspected, we will process your refund. The refund will be credited back to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking the "Sign In" button in the top-right corner of the website and selecting "Create Account". You'll need to provide your name, email address, and create a password. You can also create an account during the checkout process.

Can I send a care package to someone else?

Absolutely! Many of our customers are parents, friends, or relatives sending care packages to students. During checkout, you can specify a different shipping address and even include a personalized message with your gift.

Do you offer subscription services?

Yes, we offer monthly subscription plans for regular care package deliveries. You can choose between our Monthly Essentials, Bi-Monthly Boost, or Quarterly Care packages. Subscriptions can be paused or canceled at any time from your account dashboard.

Didn't Find Your Answer?

If you couldn't find the answer to your question, our customer support team is here to help. Reach out to us through one of the options below and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
