Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our customer service line or using WhatsApp for faster assistance.

Can I request a custom care package?

Yes, we offer customization options for all our care packages. You can either modify an existing package or request a completely custom package. Simply select "Custom Package Request" in the contact form subject and provide details of what you're looking for.

Do you offer bulk orders for university events?

Absolutely! We work with universities, student unions, and societies to provide bulk orders for events, welcome weeks, and student support initiatives. Please select "Partnership Opportunity" in the contact form and our partnerships team will get in touch to discuss your requirements and provide a quote.

How can I track my order or change my delivery address?

For order tracking and delivery address changes, please select "Order Status" in the contact form subject. Make sure to include your order number and the new delivery address if applicable. Alternatively, you can call our customer service line for immediate assistance.
