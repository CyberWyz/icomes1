We Help Students Thrive

UniEssentials was founded in 2019 by a group of recent university graduates who recognized the challenges that students face when transitioning to university life. We understand the stress of moving away from home, often for the first time, and the difficulties of managing studies, social life, and self-care.

Our mission is to make university life easier, healthier, and more enjoyable by providing carefully curated care packages that address the unique needs of students. Whether it's nutritious snacks for late-night study sessions, personal care essentials for shared accommodations, or comfort items to make a dorm room feel like home, we've got students covered.

What sets us apart is our first-hand understanding of student life. Our team has experienced the challenges of university and we design our products with those experiences in mind. We work closely with current students to ensure our packages remain relevant and valuable.

Our Mission & Values

At UniEssentials, our mission is to support students throughout their university journey by providing thoughtfully curated care packages that address their unique needs. We believe that when students have the essentials they need, they can focus on what truly matters: learning, growing, and making the most of their university experience.

Student-Centered

We put students at the heart of everything we do. Our products are designed by students, for students, ensuring they truly meet their needs.

Sustainability

We are committed to reducing our environmental impact by using eco-friendly packaging and partnering with sustainable suppliers.

Community

We believe in fostering a sense of community among students. Our packages include items that promote connection and well-being.

Our Journey

2019

The Beginning

UniEssentials was founded by Lonah Muregwi after her own challenging first year at university. The first care packages were assembled in her university dorm room.

2020

Expansion & Growth

We moved to our first warehouse and expanded our team to meet growing demand. We introduced our popular Exam Season Bundle during this year.

2021

Sustainability Focus

We committed to making our operations more sustainable by switching to eco-friendly packaging and partnering with local suppliers.

2022

New Product Lines

We launched our Wellness Collection, focusing on mental health and self-care products for students, which quickly became one of our bestsellers.

2023

Today & Beyond

We continue to grow and innovate, with plans to expand our product range and reach more universities across Harare Province and eventually Zimbabwe.
